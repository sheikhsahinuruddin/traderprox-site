Is this for beginners?

Yes. Part 1 starts from zero — what a candlestick is, what a timeframe means. From there it builds step by step into advanced topics.

Stocks or crypto — which market is this for?

The concepts — price action, entry-exit, risk management — work the same across every market: stocks, crypto, forex, commodities. Most examples use Indian stock market charts.
